TISHISHUGU

Hello! My name is Angel, I'm the designer and artist of Tishishugu (tee-sheh-shoe-goo). The name Tishishugu is derived from the phrase 'angel fish tank' in chinese, using the pinyin of each word's first chinese character. I view the world of my art and design works as my own fish tank which is why the name is so specific. This is my website and on here I've provided some of my past designs and artworks, hope you enjoy :)
